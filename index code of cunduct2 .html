Code of conduct

Please read these rules carefully. To continue using one‑to‑one sessions, you must agree to follow them in every class.

Respect and safety

  • Use polite and respectful language. No abuse, bullying, or insults in chat or audio/video.
  • Do not share hateful, violent, or adult content in messages, files, or screen share.
  • Follow the teacher’s instructions and do not disturb the session for others.

Privacy and recording

  • Do not record, screenshot, or share the session without permission from the teacher and your school.
  • Do not share your or others’ personal details (phone, address, social media IDs) in chat.

Fair use

  • Use the platform only for learning; do not spam messages, links, or files.
  • Report any harmful or suspicious behavior using the “Report” button instead of replying aggressively.
